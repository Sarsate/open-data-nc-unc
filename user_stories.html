User Stories
Requests
Request Data

Ernie is working on an article related to restaurant health codes. After doing some quick research he cannot find what he believes should be public information related to ranking and health code violations. Ernie cannot find the information himself, and wants to request that someone else locate it for him.

Adding (initial bounty)

Ernie has made a request for data on restaurants which he really needs for his article. He is willing to add a financial incentive to encourage researches to find his info.

Adding Bounty associated with a deadline

Ernies article is due in two weeks. He needs the info within ten days and is willing to pay $10 for it. He would like to let suppliers know when he needs the information.

Adding bounty to some one elses requests

Elmo, a user of the site, sees a request for information that he also needs and does not have time to find on his own. Elmo wants to add some of his own money onto the existing bounty, to better entice a researcher.

Fork a request for diffrent format

Elmo wants the information that Ernie has requested however instead of in a pdf he needs it in a cvs format. Elmo forks Erines request and adds his own bounty for the information in his format.

Forking a request to have a earlier deadline

Elmo wants the information that Erine has requested however he wants it sooner. He forks Erines request to have his own bounty with the ealier deadline.

Providing
Claim bounty

Bert, a researcher, wants to take his boyfriend out to dinner and could use some extra cash for their anniversary. He goes to the site and finds a bounty that would suffice annd which has a single (default) deadline. Bert wants to claim the request publicly so that another researcher can't take it.

Loses a Claim

Bert has claimed a request with a promise to get the information at a late deadline. He knows this means that another researcher Bert receives an email that a bounty he committed to has been claimed at an earlier deadline by someone else.

Personas
Ernie is a young and ambitious student journalist in the Triangle interning with ReeseNews. Ernie is always working hard, trying to balance his time between work, school, friends, adn practicing guitar for his band. After graduation Ernie wants to start his own non-profit news organization with a focus on government transparency and uncensored data. Ernie has little time on his hands, and needs to rely on quick and efficient resources for his research.

Bert just graduation from UNC with a major in Political Science and a minor in Philosophy. Berty lives in an apartment with 2 graduate students in Chapel Hill. ALthough Bert has had several internships with the Orang County local government, Bert is having difficulty securing a job. While Ernie is looking for a full-time job, he works as a barista at Starbucks to help pay the bills. Ernie loves to play video games, and goes hiking in his free time.

Elmo is a middle-aged government employee working for the Camden county public offices. Elmo has a wife and 3 kids, and has spent most of his years working in public policy to provide for his family. In his free time, Elmo likes to go sailing and skeet-shooting. A veteran in his department, Elmo has built several connections between county offices. Disgruntled with the direction of his small county, Elmo wants to find ways to use his experiences to influence and improve local government agencies.
